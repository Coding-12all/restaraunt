Vada

Vada is a popular South Indian snack made from a batter consisting primarily of urad dal (black gram) and spices. The batter is prepared by soaking urad dal and grinding it to a smooth consistency along with spices like green chilies, ginger, and curry leaves.

chapathi

Chapati, also known as roti, is a staple Indian flatbread made from whole wheat flour, water, and a pinch of salt. It is a versatile accompaniment to various Indian dishes and is enjoyed across the country.

idli

Idli is a traditional South Indian steamed rice cake made from a fermented batter of rice and black lentils (urad dal). The rice and lentils are soaked, ground to a smooth paste, and allowed to ferment overnight.
